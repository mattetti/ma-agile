Whatwe value

Business Value

We believe we should align our development resources to your business objectives. This involves understanding the operations of your company well enough to justify internal improvements.


Communication

Daily communication is the key to keeping a project under control.


Agility & Flexibility

Because no one can predict the future, we believe we have to be as flexible as possible. We don't believe in fixed specs. We want the solution we develop for you to adapt to the market's needs.


Rapid Application Development

We believe in dividing the life-cycle of a project into small/manageable development iterations (chunks). By using an iterative development and finding compromises between usability, features, and/or execution speed, our customers end up getting to use their product earlier and therefore saving a lot valuable resources and gaining more meaningful feedback.


Best Practices

We believe that using coding best practices greatly reduces the probability of introducing errors into our customers' applications. It also helps with keeping a coding standard for our team (BDD with RSpec, Capistrano deployment etc...).


Simplicity

Because we know too well that feature-creeping is a reality, we want to help you keep things simple. We believe in powerful yet simple solutions. The guys at 37signals have written a nice book (available online) covering this concept at length.
